Frequently Asked Questions

Is ImageResizer Pro really free?

Yes! ImageResizer Pro is completely free to use. We don't charge for any features, and there are no hidden costs or subscription fees.

Are my images safe and private?

Absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP. You can also convert between formats during the resizing process.

Is there a file size limit?

We support images up to 10MB in size. This covers most use cases while ensuring optimal performance in your browser.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Currently, we process one image at a time to ensure the best quality and performance. Batch processing is on our roadmap for future updates.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes! ImageResizer Pro is fully responsive and works great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. The interface adapts to your screen size.
