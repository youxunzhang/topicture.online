Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our text to image tool

How do I use the text to image converter?

It's very simple! Just enter the text you want to convert in the text box, choose your font, color, and background style, then click the "Generate Image" button. The generated image can be downloaded immediately.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPEG, and WebP formats. PNG is ideal for images requiring transparency, JPEG for photo-like images, and WebP provides better compression rates.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our text to image converter is completely free to use, no registration required, and no usage limits. You can use all features anytime.

How do I create social media graphics?

Choose appropriate font size and color, enter your text content, select suitable background color or image, then generate and download. We recommend using PNG format for best quality.

How do I convert quotes to images?

Paste your favorite quote in the text box, choose an elegant font (like Georgia or Times New Roman), adjust font size and color, add a suitable background, then generate the image.

Does it support Chinese text?

Absolutely! Our tool perfectly supports Chinese, English, and many other languages. All fonts support Chinese character display.

How do I create poster designs?

Enter poster title and content text, choose eye-catching font and color combinations, set appropriate background, adjust font size to fit poster dimensions, then generate high-quality images.

Is my data secure?

Very secure! All text processing is done locally in your browser. We don't collect or store any of your text content. Your privacy is completely protected.

How do I optimize image quality?

Choose higher image quality settings (90%+), use PNG format for best clarity, ensure text size is appropriate, and avoid making text too small which can cause blur.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our tool uses responsive design and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers. The interface automatically adapts to different screen sizes.
