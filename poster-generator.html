FAQ

Everything you need to know before exporting your poster.

Can I edit fonts or sizes manually?

No. Font sizes are auto-calculated to keep every poster balanced and centered.

Does the exported PNG match the selected size?

Yes. The PNG uses the exact pixel dimensions of the preset you select.

Can I use these posters commercially?

Absolutely. The included Google Fonts are free for commercial use.

Do you store my text or posters?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser and nothing is uploaded.
