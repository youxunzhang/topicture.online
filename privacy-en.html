Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

1. Information Collection

TextToImage text to image converter is committed to protecting your privacy. Our service uses local processing, which means:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any text content you input
  • All text-to-image processing is completed locally in your browser
  • We do not require you to register an account or provide personal information

2. Technical Information

We may collect the following technical information to improve our service:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system information
  • Access time and page views
  • Error logs (without personal data)

3. Cookie Usage

We use Google Analytics to analyze website usage. These services may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics - Website analytics
  • Google Fonts - Font services
  • Font Awesome - Icon services

5. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • All processing is done locally on the client side
  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • No storage of any user-generated content
  • Regular security updates and maintenance

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not specifically targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on the website and the "Last updated" date will be revised.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:
