FAQ

Quick answers before you remove a watermark.

Does this tool use AI to erase watermarks?

No. It crops or covers the selected area, so you always control the result.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens in your browser to keep files private.

Can I blur sensitive areas instead?

Yes. Choose Cover/Blur to mask sensitive content without cropping the image.

What file types can I export?
